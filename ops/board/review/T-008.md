## Why

commits onto the integrate branch and typing throwaway `merge <ID>` messages — the exact history
the clean-history model kills. The role file is the recipe agents actually execute, so until it

task → qa. Everything else the Integrator owns (risk:high human gate, flake rule, sweep, promote,
Learned log) is untouched.
